About This Library

This digital library houses works of speculative fiction that explore alternate histories and reimagine our world through different lenses.

"History is not what happened, but what survives the shipwrecks of time and the distortions of the victors."

Each book in this collection asks a different "what if" question, exploring how small changes in history could lead to dramatically different presents. From Uganda built by thinkers instead of soldiers, to time travelers attempting to change the course of African history, these stories invite us to reconsider the paths we've taken.
